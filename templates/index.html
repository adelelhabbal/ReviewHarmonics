About

When we came up with this idea, it was for a website that could tell a curious entrepreneur or innovator what they should be trying to incorporate into their idea, based on what people have expressed that they want.

Review Harmonics provides helpful details about restaurants around the world using AI. It could be used for research in building your own restaurant or just for finding out a good place to go out for food on a nice weekend.
